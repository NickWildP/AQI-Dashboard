What is AQI?

AQI (Air Quality Index) is a numerical scale used to communicate how polluted the air is.

What does a high AQI value indicate?

A high AQI value indicates that the air quality is poor and may pose health risks.

How is AQI calculated?

The AQI is calculated based on the concentration of various pollutants in the air.

What are PM2.5 and PM10?

PM2.5 and PM10 refer to particulate matter suspended in the air.
